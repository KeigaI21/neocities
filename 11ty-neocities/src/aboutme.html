About me


Hello, I'm Keiga. I draw and do other stuff too. I'm a big fan of light, weather, the sun, the moon, the sky, stars, space, the beauty of the natural world. I made this site to showcase my art in high quality and large resolution, something I can't do on Instagram. I'm proud of how far my art has come, and would like to share it with you. I hope you enjoy what I've made.
